Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens in a Discovery Session?+
A relaxed, no-pressure 30-minute conversation where we explore where you are, what's calling you, and whether we're a good fit to work together.
Do I need to know my purpose already?+
Not at all. Most clients come because they feel a sense of misalignment or curiosity. The process is designed to uncover your purpose together.
Are sessions online or in-person?+
Sessions are held via video call, so you can join from anywhere in the world. In-person intensives can be arranged upon request.
What if I can't afford the full price?+
I offer a sliding scale for students and those facing financial hardship. Reach out and we'll find something that works.
Can I add extras to any package?+
Absolutely. You can mix and match any add-on with any package. The booking form lets you select your main service and check any add-ons you'd like.
